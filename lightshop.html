Light Shop E-commerce

In the context of my second-year college project for the Web 1, I designed and developed "Light Shop" is a fashionable e-commerce store that offers a diverse range of clothing items to meet the various needs and preferences of its customers. The store is well-known for its extensive collection of clothing items, which includes jeans, shorts, t-shirts, sweaters, vests, coats, and hoodies.
